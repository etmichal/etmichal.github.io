ABOUT
ME

Hello world! I'm Erik Michal. I'm a curious mind from Washington Michigan who grew up with an adoration for technology, creativity and discovery.

In a world that modern technology has made extremely small, I've always gravitated to ways mankind can still discover new things, ideas and places. This is what lead me into design.

Having the ability to give birth to an entirely new idea or concept on a daily basis motivates me to push the boundaries of what is known and what has yet to be attempted or discovered.

Many of my favorite experiments come in the form of geometric shapes. With a background in architectural drafting, I've learned how the collimation of simple shapes and forms can create.

A complex visual puzzle. When used sparingly they can create minimalistic, spacious visual experiences that have an oddly serene tone.

Through my journey as a student and a professional I continue to adapt and evolve. I am always seeking a new challenge or opportunity that allows me a new perspective into my work and abilities.
